About this song

A Harrison-penned track from Abbey Road, destined to be a sure sign that the sun is actually gracing England with its presence for the foreseeable future.

Following a period of disillusionment with the music industry, he skipped a day at Apple’s offices and went to his good mate Eric Clapton’s house and, feeling free and back in love with music, wrote this here song.
